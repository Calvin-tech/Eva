Who We Are

Evangelist Jerry Adonai Ministries is dedicated to preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom, revealing the message of dominion, and equipping believers for impactful evangelism.

Vision & Mission

Mission

To reach the world with the Good News through evangelism, mentorship, empowerment, and sending forth disciples — raising a generation that will transform nations and advance God's Kingdom in every sphere of life.

Vision

We exist to preach the Gospel of the Kingdom so that men and women may encounter Jesus, embrace the message of dominion, and rise to walk in true Kingdom authority.

About the Founder

Evangelist Jerry Adonai is a servant of God with a burning passion to see lives transformed through the Gospel of the Kingdom. Called to reveal the message of dominion and the reality of Kingdom living, he carries a unique evangelistic mandate to raise men and women who will walk in divine authority and impact nations for Christ.

Through teaching, preaching, and outreach, Evangelist Jerry Adonai has committed himself to winning souls, mentoring believers, and equipping them for effective evangelism. His ministry emphasizes prayer, discipleship, empowerment, and the release of believers into their God-given assignments.

Driven by a vision to see multitudes reconciled to Christ and mobilized as end-time harvesters, he continues to pursue the Great Commission with passion, humility, and an unwavering focus on Kingdom advancement.
